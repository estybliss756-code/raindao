# 🌳 RainDAO - Rainforest Defense Cooperative

RainDAO is a decentralized autonomous organization focused on global forest conservation and environmental protection. Our smart contract system enables transparent governance, community contributions, and coordinated forest defense initiatives.

## 🌿 Mission

To create a global cooperative that empowers communities, environmental organizations, and individuals to protect and restore rainforests worldwide through decentralized governance and transparent resource allocation.

## ⚡ Core Features

### Governance System
- **Democratic Voting**: Members vote on conservation proposals and resource allocation
- **Weighted Voting Power**: Based on contribution history and stake in the DAO
- **Proposal Creation**: Any member can submit conservation projects for community approval
- **Transparent Decision Making**: All governance actions recorded on-chain

### Contribution Tracking
- **Environmental Impact Scoring**: Track carbon sequestration, biodiversity preservation
- **Contribution Rewards**: Members earn tokens based on verified conservation activities
- **Project Funding**: Direct funding for approved rainforest protection initiatives
- **Impact Verification**: Community-based verification of conservation efforts

### Member Management
- **Membership Tiers**: Different levels based on contribution and commitment
- **Reputation System**: Build credibility through verified conservation work
- **Stake Management**: Lock tokens to demonstrate long-term commitment
- **Reward Distribution**: Fair allocation based on impact and participation

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture

### Smart Contracts

#### `raindao-governance.clar`
Core governance contract handling:
- Proposal creation and voting
- Member registration and management
- Voting power calculation
- Treasury management

#### `forest-contributions.clar`
Conservation tracking contract managing:
- Environmental impact recording
- Contribution verification
- Reward calculation and distribution
- Project milestone tracking

### Key Functions

- `register-member`: Join the DAO as a conservation advocate
- `create-proposal`: Submit conservation projects for community vote
- `cast-vote`: Participate in democratic decision making
- `record-contribution`: Log environmental conservation activities
- `claim-rewards`: Receive tokens for verified conservation work
- `verify-impact`: Community verification of environmental projects

## 🌍 Environmental Impact

RainDAO focuses on measurable conservation outcomes:

- **Forest Area Protected**: Track hectares under community protection
- **Carbon Sequestration**: Monitor CO2 absorption from protected forests  
- **Biodiversity Preservation**: Document species and ecosystem protection
- **Community Engagement**: Number of local communities participating
- **Reforestation Projects**: New forest areas established through DAO funding

## 🚀 Getting Started

### For Conservationists
1. Register as a DAO member
2. Stake tokens to gain voting power
3. Participate in governance decisions
4. Submit or support conservation proposals

### For Local Communities
1. Partner with RainDAO for forest protection
2. Receive funding for conservation projects
3. Report impact and receive rewards
4. Build long-term sustainability programs

### For Developers
1. Clone this repository
2. Install Clarinet development environment
3. Run `clarinet check` to validate contracts
4. Deploy to testnet for experimentation
